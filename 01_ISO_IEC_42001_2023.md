## What it is
An international management-system standard for governing AI across an organization.

## What it covers
- AI governance structure and accountability
- AI lifecycle controls (design, build, deploy, operate)
- Risk, impact, and compliance management
- Monitoring, auditability, continual improvement

## Why it matters
This is the primary ISO anchor for enterprise AI governance, similar in role to ISO 27001 for information security.
