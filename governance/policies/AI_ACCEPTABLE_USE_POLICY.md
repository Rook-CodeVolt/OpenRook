# AI Acceptable Use Policy (Template)

## 1) Purpose
This policy defines acceptable and prohibited use of AI and agent capabilities to ensure safe, lawful, and accountable operation.

## 2) Scope
Applies to:
- employees, contractors, and third parties using AI/agents
- AI systems, models, agents, and supporting integrations
- all environments (dev/test/staging/prod)

## 3) Roles and responsibilities
- **Policy Owner:** approves and maintains this policy.
- **System Owner:** ensures implementation and evidence.
- **Security/Compliance:** monitors control adherence.
- **Users/Operators:** use AI/agents within authorized boundaries.

## 4) Permitted use
- Approved business workflows and documented use cases.
- Approved data classes as defined by data classification policy.
- Approved tools/integrations under least-privilege controls.
- Approved autonomy tier for the given role/use case.

## 5) Prohibited use
- Processing restricted data without explicit authorization.
- Bypassing logging, approvals, or policy controls.
- External communications or transactions outside approved workflow.
- Using unapproved models, credentials, or shadow tooling.
- Prompting systems to circumvent controls or hide actions.

## 6) Data handling requirements
- Data minimization by default.
- Sensitive data redaction where feasible.
- Encryption in transit and at rest.
- Retention and deletion per legal and policy requirements.

## 7) Human oversight requirements
- High-risk outputs require human review before action.
- Safety-critical decisions must not be fully autonomous without explicit governance approval.

## 8) Monitoring and evidence
Required evidence:
- user/action logs,
- policy decision logs (allow/deny),
- approval records,
- incident records and corrective actions.

## 9) Violations and exceptions
- Violations may result in access removal, disciplinary action, or escalation.
- Exceptions require written approval, compensating controls, and expiry date.

## 10) Review cadence
- Minimum annual review, or sooner on major regulatory/technical change.
