# AI Acceptable Use Policy (Template)

## Purpose
Set permitted and prohibited use of AI and agent capabilities.

## Permitted use
- approved business workflows
- approved data classes and integrations
- role-authorized autonomous actions

## Prohibited use
- processing prohibited/sensitive data without approval
- bypassing controls, audit, or approval gates
- autonomous external actions outside policy scope

## Responsibilities
- Users: follow policy and report incidents
- Owners: maintain controls and evidence
- Security/Governance: monitor compliance and exceptions
